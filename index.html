Australia's Tourism in 2025

This visualisation explores international and domestic tourism in Australia using data from Tourism Research Australia.

Top 10 Countries Visiting Australia

New Zealand is the biggest source of international visitors, followed by China and the UK.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

Why Do People Visit Australia?

Most visitors come for a holiday, but visiting friends and relatives is also very common.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

International Visitor Spend by State (2025)

NSW leads, driven largely by Sydney. WA saw the strongest growth at +23%.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

International vs Domestic Overnight Trips by State

Domestic trips far outnumber international visits in every state. NSW and Victoria dominate both categories.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey & Domestic Tourism Statistics, 2025

Where Do International Visitors Stay?

Rented apartments and friends/relatives properties are the most common accommodation types.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

Top 20 Regions Visited by International Tourists

Sydney dominates by a large margin. Melbourne and Brisbane follow, with regional areas also attracting significant visitors.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025
