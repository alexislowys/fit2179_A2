Australia's Tourism in 2025

This visualisation explores international and domestic tourism in Australia using data from Tourism Research Australia and the Australian Bureau of Statistics.

Top 10 Countries Visiting Australia

New Zealand is the biggest source of international visitors, followed by China and the UK. Together they make up nearly half of all international trips to Australia.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

Why Do People Visit Australia?

Most international visitors come for a holiday, but visiting friends and relatives is also very common. Education is a significant category too, reflecting Australia's large international student population.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

International Visitor Spend by State (2025)

NSW attracts the most international spend, largely driven by Sydney. Western Australia saw the strongest growth in 2025, up 23% in visitor numbers compared to 2024.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, International Visitor Survey, Year ending December 2025

Domestic Overnight Trips by State (Dec Quarter 2025)

Australian residents took 29.5 million overnight trips in the December quarter 2025. NSW and Victoria dominate domestic travel.

Source: Tourism Research Australia, Domestic Tourism Statistics, December Quarter 2025
